### TIMELINE AND CHRONOLOGY

1. **Mark's suicide timing.** The timeline says "exact timing to be determined---could be late teens or early twenties." This affects the emotional register of at least four chapters (Ch. 4, 5, 6, 16) and determines whether Olivia processes it as an adolescent or a young adult. If late teens (~17), it lands before college and colors the Jesuit chapters with fresh grief. If early twenties, it overlaps with the modeling/Hollywood period and changes the meaning of her self-destructive behavior.

2. **Chapter count discrepancy.** The master overview says "24 chapters across 5 parts." The chapter outline contains 27 chapters. Which is correct? If 27, update the master overview. If 24, identify which chapters should be merged or cut.

Several events have flexible placement (Mark enters family: 6--8; boat accident: ~11; go-kart: ~12).
